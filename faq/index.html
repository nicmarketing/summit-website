Services
Summit Marketing MS is a full-service digital marketing agency based in Mississippi. We help businesses grow online through SEO, content marketing, brand strategy, social media management, email automation, and conversion rate optimization. We also build and optimize websites built to convert. Every strategy we build is tied directly to measurable outcomes — leads, revenue, and growth.
Most agencies sell deliverables — posts, reports, and hours. We sell outcomes. Every plan at Summit Marketing is built around measurable growth: more traffic, more leads, more revenue. We use AI-powered tools and automation to move faster without sacrificing quality. You get a dedicated strategist who knows your business, not an account manager cycling through a spreadsheet. And unlike most agencies, we're not afraid to tell you when something isn't working — and change course fast.
We work with businesses across a wide range of industries including local service businesses, professional services (law, finance, healthcare), e-commerce brands, restaurants, real estate, and B2B companies. Our strategies are tailored to your specific market and customer — we don't use cookie-cutter templates.
Our current retainer plans focus on organic growth strategies — SEO, content, email, and social media. We deliberately built our packages this way because organic channels build compounding, long-term value that paid ads can't replicate on their own. That said, if paid advertising is a strong fit for your goals, we can discuss it as part of a custom Peak-tier engagement.
Full-funnel marketing means we cover every stage of your customer's journey — from the moment they first hear about you (awareness) to when they become a paying customer (conversion) and beyond (retention). Most agencies only focus on one or two stages. With our Ascent and Peak plans, we build a connected system that moves people from strangers to loyal customers — and keeps them there.
Yes. While we're headquartered in Mississippi and love supporting local businesses, we work with clients across the United States. Digital marketing is borderless — if you're looking to grow online, we can help regardless of where you're located.

Web Design & Development
Yes. Web design and development is a standalone service we offer at Summit Marketing MS. Our three tiers cover everything from clean, professional brochure sites to fully custom e-commerce builds. Every site we build is conversion-focused, SEO-ready, and optimized for Core Web Vitals from day one. See web design pricing →
Trailhead ($300–$2K) is ideal for new businesses or personal brands that need a clean, professional online presence — typically 5–10 pages, template-based with custom branding applied.

Pinnacle ($2K–$5K) is built for established small businesses needing a fully customized design, professional copywriting structure, and advanced on-page SEO.

Apex ($5K–$20K+) is our enterprise-grade tier for e-commerce stores, complex platforms, large inventories, or businesses that need custom functionality and integrations.
Timelines vary by scope. A Trailhead site typically launches in 1–2 weeks. A Pinnacle build takes 3–5 weeks from kickoff to launch. Apex projects vary widely depending on complexity but usually run 6–14 weeks. We provide a detailed timeline at the start of every project so there are no surprises.
Always.
